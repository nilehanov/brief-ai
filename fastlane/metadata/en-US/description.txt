Brief is your AI-powered meeting companion that helps you prepare before and debrief after every meeting. Powered entirely by Apple Intelligence, all processing happens on your device -- your meeting data never leaves your iPhone or iPad.

BEFORE THE MEETING
Paste your meeting agenda or context, and Brief instantly generates:
- Key talking points to raise
- Smart questions to ask
- Potential objections to anticipate
- Suggested meeting structure
- Time allocation recommendations

AFTER THE MEETING
Paste your raw notes, and Brief extracts:
- Key decisions made
- Action items with owners and deadlines
- Open questions that need follow-up
- A ready-to-send follow-up email

FOUR SPECIALIZED TEMPLATES
- Meeting Prep: Full preparation materials for any meeting
- Meeting Debrief: Structured output from meeting notes
- 1:1 Prep: Thoughtful conversation starters and topics
- Presentation Prep: Key messages, slide structure, and opening hooks

COMPLETE PRIVACY
Brief uses Apple Intelligence for all AI processing. Your meeting data, notes, and generated content are processed entirely on-device and stored only in your local app data. Nothing is ever sent to external servers.

EXPORT & SHARE
- Copy follow-up emails directly to your clipboard
- Export meeting briefs as professional PDFs
- Share results with your team

Requires an Apple Intelligence-capable device (iPhone 16 or later, iPad with M-series chip) running iOS 26 or later.
